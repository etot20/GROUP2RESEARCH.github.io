SimBio Labs creates innovative active learning tools for college-level biology courses, integrating interactive simulations that promote critical thinking and exploration of biological concepts. Their modules cover areas like ecology, evolution, cell biology, and molecular biology. Designed for flexibility, they can be used as textbook replacements or supplements in various formats such as in-class activities or homework assignments. SimBio's products emphasize experiential learning by simulating experiments where students engage in inquiry and receive instant feedback. The content is research-backed and built with contributions from grant-funded studies, supported by the National Science Foundation and other organizations, to enhance educational effectiveness.
